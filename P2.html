Greenpeace(Page2)

Greenpeace (greenpeace), an international non-governmental organization. Formerly founded in Canada on September 15,1971," No show of hands Committee ", changed to Green Peace in 1979, headquartered in Amsterdam, the Netherlands.

Mission: to protect the safety and sustainable development of the earth, the environment and its various organisms, and to make positive changes with action. "Promote solutions conducive to environmental protection, whether in scientific research or scientific inventions. The aim is to promote a greener, peaceful and sustainable future.

Greenpeace has branches in more than 40 countries and regions around the world, with more than 3 million supporters. In order to maintain impartiality and independence, Greenpeace does not from any government, business or political group, but only direct contributions from citizens and independent funds.
